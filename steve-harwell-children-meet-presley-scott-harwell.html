Steve Harwell children: Meet Presley Scott Harwell

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Presley Scott Harwell was the only son of Steven Scott Harwell who died at the age of 56, on September 4, 2023, and he was an American singer. Harwell served as the lead vocalist and frontman for the rock band Smash Mouth from its founding in 1994 until his retirement in 2021. Before his retirement, he and bassist Paul De Lisle were the only two constant members of the band.

Harwell was also a rapper in F.O.S. and was a featured cast member in the sixth season of the VH1 reality show The Surreal Life in 2006. He has appeared on other television and radio shows, as well as making a cameo in the 2001 film Rat Race. He is best known for the song “All-Star”

Steve Harwell children: Meet Presley Scott Harwell

Presley Scott Harwell is the son of Steve Harwell and his wife Michelle Laroque. He was the only child of his parents and was born on January 6, 2001. Presley unfortunately passed away in July 2001 from acute lymphocytic leukemia at the age of six months; subsequently, Harwell created a medical research fund in Presley’s name.
