Biography:

Cody Longo was born on March 4, 1988 in Littleton, Colorado. He made his film debut as Eddie in Hip Hop Kidz: It’s a Beautiful Thing. He has since starred in other films like Fame, High School, Piranha 3D and For the Love of Money.

He then went on to make his television debut in Medium. From 2009 to 2010, he became known for his recurring role as Nicky Russo in Make It or Break It.

He has had guest roles in such television shows as Brothers & Sisters, Days of Our Lives and CSI: NY before securing the main role of Eddie Duran in Hollywood Heights.
