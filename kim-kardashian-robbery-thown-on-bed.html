Kim Kardashian Remembers Being Thrown on the Bed When She Was Robbed in Paris

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Kim Kardashian recalls more and more of her robbery in detail in latest preview for Sunday's episode of Keeping Up with the Kardashians. The reality TV star remembers being home in the hotel while her sisters Kourtney Kardashian and Kendall Jenner were out, accompanied by the family body guard Pascal Duvier.

That October night in Paris, she was dozing off to sleep when she heard a pounding noise from the stairs and banging on the door, she says in the teaser. She assumed it was just her sister Kourtney and assistant Stephanie Sheppard coming home drunk. But when she called out "Hello?" to the door and no one answered, that's when she realized she was in trouble. "My heart started to get really tense," Kardashian says. "I knew something wasn't quite right."

Getty Images

Bodyguard Pascal Duvier with Kim Kardashian in Paris before her robbery.

According to her account, this is what happened next: outside her bedroom door, "five feet away," she saw two men dressed in police uniforms holding another man down. So she slid off her bed, pulled out her phone and tried calling Duvier, since she didn't know how to call 911 in France, but she wasn't able to make the call in time.

"The guy came in, grabbed the phone from me, threw me on the bed, and I was like: this is it," she recalls.

She also remembers seeing the concierge to the hotel handcuffed and holding the key to her room. He later told her that the robbers had asked him, "Where's the rapper's wife?" and "Let us up to her room" in French.
