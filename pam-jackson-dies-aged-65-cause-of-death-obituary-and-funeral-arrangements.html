Pam Jackson dies aged 65: Cause of death, obituary and funeral arrangements

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Pam Jackson has died at the age of 65. She was a longtime executive of the former 20th Century Fox International Distribution division of 21st Century Fox.

Pam Jackson passed away on September 27 in Los Angeles, surrounded by her family, after a long battle with cancer.

Pam Jackson Cause of death

Pam Jackson died as a result of cancer. She was diagnosed with cancer some months before her death, but there were not enough details about her health issues.

Pam Jackson’s obituary and funeral arrangements

Pam Jackson was an Associate Producer of Ally McBeal, working behind the scenes on the Emmy-winning hit from its pilot episode to its series finale five years later. She began her career working for TV legend Aaron Spelling.

Pam Jackson served as Vice President of publicity and Marketing for the Fox television studio’s international distribution team. She took a voluntary buyout from the Walt Disney Company shortly after it acquired that company.

Pam Jackson is survived by her sister Debra and brother Kenneth, as well as nephews and nieces Damian Howard, Courtney Howard, Keana Jackson, KeAra Jackson, Kerri Chambers, and great nephew Damian Christopher Howard, as well as dozens of friends and colleagues.

Pam Jackson’s funeral and burial arrangement will be held on Thursday, November 2, 2023, at 11 a.m. at the Center of Hope, 9550 Crenshaw Blvd in Inglewood.
