Who Is Sarah Millican?

Sarah Millican is 47 years old, as of 2022. She was born on 29 May 1975, in South Shields, Tyne and Wear, England. She grew up in a working-class family and attended Westoe Secondary Modern School. After school, she worked various jobs including as a secretary and an English teacher before starting her comedy career.
