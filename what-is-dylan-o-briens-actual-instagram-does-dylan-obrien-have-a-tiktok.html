What is Dylan O Brien's actual Instagram? Does Dylan O'Brien have a TikTok?

Publish date: 2024-08-15

American actor Dylan Rhodes O’Brien was born on August 26, 1991. His first significant role was that of Stiles Stilinski on the MTV supernatural drama Teen Wolf, where he appeared in all six seasons as a series regular.

He gained greater notoriety for playing Thomas, the main character, in the science fiction film trilogy The Maze Runner, which resulted in more film roles.

O’Brien was raised in Springfield Township, New Jersey, until the age of twelve, when he and his family moved to Hermosa Beach, California. He is of Irish, Italian, English, and Spanish descent.
