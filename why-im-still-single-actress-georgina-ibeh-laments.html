Why Im Still Single Actress Georgina Ibeh Laments

Publish date: 2024-08-01

Sensational Nollywood actress and producer, Georgina Ibeh has revealed that despite being a wife material, she is still single.

In a recent interview she granted published on Kemi Filani news, Georgina Ibeh disclosed that she is a private person and wants to settle down with a man who has family at heart.

Sharing the attributes a man must before agreeing to marry him, the beautiful and curvy screen goddess intimated that she loves men who smell and look good and is hopeful she find that man after her own heart soon.

She said:

“I am still single, but I know my own man will find me when it’s time. I’m quite a private person when it comes to my relationship life. I want a man who would just be himself. He should be a good man with a family-oriented mindset. I love men who look good and smell nice. He becomes more attractive to me when I get to know his understanding about life in general.”

When asked if she has ever considered getting married to a colleague? Or maybe an entertainer? The Public Administration Graduate said,

“Well, I haven’t thought about it but if the person is good, I might. But honestly, I haven’t thought about dating or marrying an actor.”
