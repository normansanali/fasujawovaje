Jordynne Grace Says Shes Happy To Officially Call IMPACT Wrestling Home

Publish date: 2024-07-03

Jordynne Grace posted the following on her Twitter account, seemingly in response to the recent report that she was working as a free agent while appearing for IMPACT Wrestling.

Sometimes happiness isn’t a feeling. It’s a decision. Many paths have been placed in front of me during the past 24 hours, but just one place I feel I truly belong. Happy to officially call @IMPACTWRESTLING home for years to come.

Exposure and money are absolutely worthless if you aren’t happy. I’ll never apologize for putting my contentment first.

PWInsider reported Grace was not signed to a contract with IMPACT Wrestling despite it being believed she signed a two-year deal in October. The report had also noted that IMPACT was “scrambling” to get Grace to signed, and if that was unsuccessful, she would likely cease working for the company. Now that Grace has commented, it looks like she’s not going anywhere.
