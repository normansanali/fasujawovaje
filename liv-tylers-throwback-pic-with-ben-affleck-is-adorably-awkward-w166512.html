Liv Tylers Throwback Pic With Ben Affleck Is Adorably Awkward

Publish date: 2024-09-12

Could stay lost in this moment forever! Liv Tyler shared the best throwback photo featuring herself and Ben Affleck on Monday, March 7. The actress flashed back to the stars’ 1998 movie Armageddon via Instagram, and the snapshot instantly went viral.

“Ohhh Monday …. @benaffleck #armageddon #benaffleck #1998,” the pregnant star, 38, captioned the black-and-white snapshot.

In the pic, Affleck, 43 — in a dirty, sweaty tank top — is awkwardly biting down on Tyler’s head. Tyler embraces the hunky star as she grabs onto his waist.

Tyler and Affleck play engaged couple Grace Stamper and A.J. Frost in the Michael Bay-directed disaster sci-fi flick. Grace’s dad, Harry Stamper (Bruce Willis), isn’t a fan of A.J.’s, but the two men work together to stop an asteroid from destroying the Earth. (Cue the tears!)
