Who is Hayden Hopkins?

Not to mention, Hayden Hopkins is an amazing dancer & choreographer who has caught the eyes of many in her work with Cirque Du Soleil. Hayden was born with an innate love for dance, and has been perfecting her craft since early childhood — she started winning titles at the youngest levels of competition; and appeared on the covers of Dance Spirit and Dance Informa magazine by age 19. Hopkins commitment to dance took her to New York City, where she studied dance and started her own line of fashionable and functional dancewear, HHxx before moving on to Las Vegas.

What is the Deal?

Her public statement demonstrates Hayden's unflinching and undeniable perseverance and authenticity under fire from some of the harshest media scrutiny there is.


The Enigma of Hayden Hopkins

Hayden Hopkins will forever be a riddle, balancing the complexities of superstardom and the media spotlight with poise and grace. Hayden Hopkins is one of the courageous and hardworking warriors with a backstory from an emerging dancer to become a well-known face.
