Who is Naomi Judd's husband Larry Strickland?

Publish date: 2024-07-25

LARRY Strickland has had a stellar career ever since he started performing with Elvis Presley in 1974.

He and his late wife Naomi Judd made great hits in their respective music careers.

Who is Naomi Judd's husband Larry Strickland?

According to his official website, Larry Strickland was born in Raleigh, North Carolina.

He initially started singing gospel quartets traveling and performing in different places in North Carolina.

After he served in the US Army from 1966 to 1970, he came back to perform in his home state until he was noticed by JD Sumner and the Stamps Quartet.

After Larry joined their group in 1974, he started touring with Elvis Presley.

He and Naomi Judd had been married since 1989, until she passed away on April 30, 2022.

What happened to his wife Naomi Judd?

On Saturday, April 30, 2022, Naomi Judd passed away at the age of 76.
