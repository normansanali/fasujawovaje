Spouse

Casey DeSantis husband Ron DeSantis is the 46th Governor of Florida. Ron is serving as the governor since 8 January 2019.

Ron's parents are Karen Rogers and Ronald Daniel DeSantis. Karen was a nurse while Daniel was a rating box installer for Nelsen TV.

Born on 14 September 1978, DeSantis was raised in Jacksonville, Florida. He is an Italian American and he is proud of his heritage.

Ron grew up with his only sibling Christina Marie who passed away in 2015. He was grief-stricken by the early demise of his young sister.

Explore DeSantis Early Life

Ron graduated from Dunedin High School in 1987. Before that, he was a student at Our Lady of Lourdes Catholic School.

DeSantis joined Yale University where he studied history and became captain of the Yale Bulldogs baseball team. He also became a part of the Delta Kappa Epsilon fraternity.

During his time at Yale, Ron served as an electrician's assistant and coach at a baseball camp. In 2001, he completed his B.A degree from the university.

Following his graduation, DeSantis became a history teacher and baseball coach at Darlington School. He then attended Harvard Law School and earned a Juris Doctor.

Ron applied to the US Navy when he was at Harvard Law School. He got selected during the second year and he transferred to Naval Justice School.

Initially, DeSantis was assigned to Navy Judge Advocate General's Corps (JAG). In 2006, he was promoted from a junior officer to a lieutenant.

Ron was deployed to the Joint Task Force Guantanamo where he directly worked with the detainees. In 2007, he was stationed in Iraq as a legal advisor for Seal Team One of the Naval Special Warfare Command Group.

DeSantis returned back home in 2008 and joined Naval Region Southeast Legal Service. He was appointed as the Special Assistant U.S. Attorney at the U.S. Department of Justice. 

Ron was honorably discharged from his position in February 2010. Following that, he became lieutenant in the Judge Advocate General's Corps of The US Navy Reserve.

DeSantis won several accolades for his 15 years of service to the nation. His Navy membership ended after his gubernatorial inauguration in 2019.

DeSantis Became Active In Politics In 2012

Ron Gave A Speech After His Victory In 2022 Election ( Source : youtu )

Ron ran for Florida's 6th congressional district in 2012 and won the election with 39% votes.

He preceded Cliff Stearns and became a U.S. House of Representatives member. He held the position until 10 September 2018.

Meanwhile, DeSantis continued his job in the Navy. He announced his candidacy for the United States Senate election in 2016 but later withdrew.

Ron confirmed his candidacy for the governor of Florida in January 2018. He ran for the 2022 re-election and earned a victory.
