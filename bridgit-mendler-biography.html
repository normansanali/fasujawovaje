Who is Bridgit Mendler?

Bridgit Mendler is an American actress, singer, and songwriter.

Singer Bridgit is known for her Disney role as Juliet van Heusen in “Wizards of Waverly Place”.

She has also starred in other Disney roles like Teddy Duncan in Disney series “Good Luck Charlie”.

As a singer, she released her first album “Hello My Name Is…” in 2012.

Being a celebrity in Hollywood, she has been able to use her celeb power to attract major attention toward philanthropy and has been involved in different campaigns.

How many siblings does Bridgit Mendler have?

Bridgit Mendler was born in Washington as Bridgit Claire Mendler on December 18, 1992.

Being born in Washington, she was raised there until the age of 8. Later, her family shifted to Mill Valley, California.

Most of her acting interests grew here.

Her interest in music and acting was getting developed as she grew up.

She played minor roles in different plays and musicals. Performing from a young age, she became the youngest performer in the San Francisco Fringe Festival.

According to her, she was 11 years old when she hired an agent to pursue her acting career.

Her childhood days were pretty much spent acting and performing.

Per her education, she has a degree from the University of Southern California with a major in anthropology.

She studied Medieval Visual Culture and Medical Anthropology there.
