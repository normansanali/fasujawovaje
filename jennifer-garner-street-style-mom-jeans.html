Jennifer Garner Wears Mom Jeans While Running Errands in L.A.

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Jennifer Garner kept it casual in Los Angeles this weekend.

Yesterday, the Adam Project star was seen running errands around town and shopping for cosmetics at Smashbox.

For the low-key outing, she wore a navy T-shirt and baggy light-wash mom jeans. She finished the comfy look with gray sneakers and tortoiseshell sunglasses. Garner's hair was still wet, and she wore no makeup. Her hands full, she was seen carrying a baby blue striped sweater, a pair of glasses, and a canned soda.

Ever the private star, Garner has maintained a low profile ever since news broke that her ex-husband Ben Affleck married Jennifer Lopez in a secret ceremony in Las Vegas. On the day of their nuptials, Garner was on a relaxing solo trip at Lake Tahoe.
