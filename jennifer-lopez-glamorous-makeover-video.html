See Jennifer Lopez Glowing in This Glamorous Makeover Video

Publish date: 2024-09-04

It's not every day that Jennifer Lopez decides to not wear a nude lip—but when it happens, that day's a good one.

Yesterday, the multi-hyphenate posted a glamorous makeover video on Instagram to show off her glam, completed by makeup artist Mary Phillips and hairstylist Chris Appleton.

In the clip, the Marry Me actress glows in soft blush tones, which are highlighted across her eyelids and cheekbones. She also eschews her typical nude lip, opting for a glossy rosy shade instead. She wears her hair up in a sleek bun with a few face-framing strands left down and braided. And she also offers a sneak peek at her outfit, which includes gold circular earrings and a pink rose-print robe.

She captioned the post, "Can't touch this ✨#WeekendShoots @ChrisAppleton1 @MaryPhillips @robzangardi @marielhaenn @tombachik @jlobeauty."

Lately, J.Lo has been busy promoting her new Netflix documentary, Halftime, which follows the behind-the-scenes journey of her 2020 Super Bowl Halftime Show, as well as explores her decades-spanning career as a giant of her industry.

"Halftime offers an intimate peek behind the curtain revealing the grit and determination that makes Jennifer Lopez the icon she is, from her performances onscreen and on stages around the world, to her Super Bowl halftime show, to the recent presidential inauguration," a press release for the project stated. "The documentary focuses on an international superstar who has inspired people for decades with her perseverance, creative brilliance, and cultural contributions. And it's only the beginning. Halftime serves as the kickoff to the second half of Lopez's life, as she lays bare her evolution as a Latina, a mother, and an artist, taking agency in her career and using her voice for a greater purpose."
