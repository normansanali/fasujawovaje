Ronnie Liang - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-09-14

Pop singer who released his debut album Ronnie Liang: Ang Aking Awitin in 2007. He won Pop Male Performance on a Music Video of the Year at the ASAP Pop Viewer's Choice Awards that year. 

Before Fame

He was the second runner-up on the reality-talent search show, Pinoy Dream Academy in 2005. 

Trivia

He sang OPM's hit "Ngiti" on ABS-CBN's Sunday variety show ASAP 08. He has over 350,000 followers on his Pop Male Performance on a Music Video of the Year Instagram account. 

Family Life

He is the youngest of seven children.

Associated With

He competed on Pinoy Dream Academy with Yeng Constantino.
