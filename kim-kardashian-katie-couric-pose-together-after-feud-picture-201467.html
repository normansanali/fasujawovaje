Kim Kardashian, Katie Couric Pose Together After Feud: Picture

Publish date: 2024-07-08

The new brides are ready for a new chapter! Kim Kardashian and Katie Couric posed together while celebrating the Fourth of July in the Hamptons following their social media feud last August.

“Great night @kimkardashian @shellibird1 @christiebrinkley @katiecouric! #hamptonslife,” Kris Jenner wrote via Instagram on Saturday, July 5.

Alongside the caption, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians momager posted a picture of the famous gals and friend Shelli Azoff dolled up for the holiday. Supermodel and Hamptons queen Christie Brinkley stood between Kanye West’s wife, 33, and Couric.

In another snapshot, Brinkley posed with actress-philanthropist Marlo Thomas and Kardashian at the party. “With Two Icons…That Girl, Marlo Thomas and that other girl who needs no introduction..@kimkardashian!” she wrote. “Both were so sweet and charming! Last year, Couric was quoted as saying that she didn’t understand why the Kardashians were famous. In response, Kardashian slammed the 57-year-old veteran TV journalist for sending a set of Barney’s New York pajamas to her newborn North West. “#IHateFakeMediaFriends,” Kardashian wrote via Instagram, along with a picture of Couric’s present and congratulatory card. “#MayIHumblySuggestYouNotSendGiftsThenTalkSh*t.”
