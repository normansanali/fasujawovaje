Aespa Will Perform A Virtual Roblox Concert For Crocs 20th Anniversary

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Aespa, a K-pop girl group, has announced that they would play in a virtual concert on Roblox to commemorate 20th Croc Day. Crocs also announced the announcement on their social media channel on October 21, 2022. The performance by Aespa will begin at 5 p.m. PT. Aespa will be performing at Croc World on Roblox, which is not far from KWANGYA, according to a Korean media site. In addition, the female group will meet their fans online on Zepeto on October 24, 2022, at 5:30 p.m. KST.

More about the K-pop girl group Aespa and its brand representation

ScreaM Records, a division of SM Entertainment, has published iScreaM Vol. 18: Girls Remixes, which features two remix versions of the group’s album Girls. The first version, Girls’ BRLLNT Remix, includes peppy sounds, whilst the second version, Girls’ Minit Remix, is a hard rock mix with bass notes and guitar riffs.

Karina, Giselle, Winter, and Ningning are the four members of the band, who debuted under SM Entertainment on November 17, 2020. Black Mamba is one of the finest debuts of all time for the group. On July 5, 2022, they also attended the UN High-level Political Forum on Sustainable Development (HLPF). On October 15 and 16, 2022, the group also played at the Los Angeles K-Pop event KAMP LA 2022.

But it wasn’t the only thing. Aespa’s members wear several hats, and they recently astonished their admirers with their graphics in the November edition of Nylong China. They are also ambassadors for the Swiss luxury accessory company Chopard, and donned jewelry and watches that added a traditional and exquisite touch to their appearances.

On February 10, 2021, the female quartet was named worldwide ambassador for the prestigious Givenchy brand. They are the first K-pop group to be discovered by a French fashion business. The performers signed an endorsement deal with the Korean cosmetics company Clio in August of the same year. Mediheal is a Korean skincare brand. In September, they also signed agreements with French sportswear brand Eider and KB Kookmin Bank. The trio was named an ambassador for the Italian fragrance brand Acqua di Parma on November 23. The band is also the face of the Korean beverage company Lotte Chilsung’s Tamz Zero. They were appointed as the worldwide spokesperson for the Korean sportswear brand MLB in August 2022.
