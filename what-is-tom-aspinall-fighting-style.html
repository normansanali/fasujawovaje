What is Tom Aspinall fighting style?

Publish date: 2024-07-14

Tom Aspinall, the English professional mixed martial artist, has gained recognition for his exceptional fighting style and impressive performances in the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) heavyweight division. 

Background and Early Training

Aspinall's journey into mixed martial arts began at the age of seven when he commenced training in JuJitsu at the Leigh Self Defence Studio in Wigan, Greater Manchester, following in the footsteps of his father, who was a jiu-jitsu instructor at Team Kaobon.

Transitioning from JuJitsu to wrestling and boxing, Aspinall eventually embraced Brazilian jiu-jitsu, where he has achieved remarkable success, winning the British Open in Brazilian jiu-jitsu in various belt classes.

His transition to mixed martial arts began following his father's association with Team Kaobon, sparking his interest in the sport.

His growth from 5 feet 8 inches to 6 feet 5 inches after completing school at the age of 16 highlights his physical prowess and dedication to the sport.

UFC Career and Fighting Style

Aspinall's UFC journey has been nothing short of impressive, with a series of notable fights that showcase his exceptional fighting style and prowess in the heavyweight division.

His fighting style is characterized by a combination of skills, agility, and strategic prowess, making him a force to be reckoned with in the octagon.

Striking Ability

One of Aspinall's standout features is his striking ability, which has been evident in his fights where he has displayed remarkable precision and power.

His lightning-fast striking, coupled with his significant height advantage at 6 feet 5 inches, allows him to dominate his opponents in the stand-up game.

As observed in his fights, Aspinall's striking prowess has been a key element of his success in the UFC, enabling him to secure victories through technical knockouts.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Expertise

As a black belt in Brazilian jiu-jitsu, Aspinall's proficiency in grappling and submission techniques has been a defining aspect of his fighting style. His expertise in Brazilian jiu-jitsu has been evident in his victories via submissions in several fights.

This skill set adds a versatile dimension to his fighting style, allowing him to seamlessly transition between striking and ground game strategies, making him a well-rounded fighter in the heavyweight division.

Agility and Athleticism

Aspinall's agility and athleticism play a pivotal role in his fighting style, enabling him to swiftly maneuver in the octagon and capitalize on openings to execute his striking and grappling techniques.

His ability to move fluidly despite his imposing stature contributes to his effectiveness in both offensive and defensive maneuvers during fights, further solidifying his status as a top contender in the UFC heavyweight division.

Notable Fights and Achievements

Throughout his UFC career, Aspinall has demonstrated his fighting style in various high-profile matchups, including his victories over reputable opponents such as Alexander Volkov, Marcin Tybura, and Sergei Pavlovich.

Notably, his wins via straight armbar submission, technical knockout, and first-round knockout underscore his diverse skill set and adaptability in the octagon.

Also, his recognition with multiple "Performance of the Night" awards reflects the impact of his fighting style in delivering compelling and dominant performances.

Future Prospects and Potential Title Contention

Aspinall's remarkable return to the octagon following a year-long hiatus due to injury serves as a testament to his resilience and determination.

His aspirations to compete against the likes of Ciryl Gane, Serghei Spivac, and his expressed interest in a potential matchup with Jon Jones highlight his ambitions to ascend further in the heavyweight division.

With his formidable fighting style and consistent track record of victories, Aspinall's pursuit of UFC title contention remains a compelling narrative in the world of mixed martial arts.

Tom Aspinall's fighting style encapsulates a blend of striking prowess, Brazilian jiu-jitsu expertise, agility, and athleticism, making him a formidable force in the UFC heavyweight division.
