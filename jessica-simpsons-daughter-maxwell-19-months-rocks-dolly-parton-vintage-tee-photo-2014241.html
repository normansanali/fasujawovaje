Jessica Simpson's Daughter Maxwell Wears Dolly Parton Tee: Photo

Publish date: 2024-09-15

Maxwell's gone country! Jessica Simpson just can't stop sharing pics of her adorable 19-month-old daughter. The singer shared a sweet snapshot of her toddler rocking a Dolly Parton t-shirt on Thursday, Jan. 23.

"Maxi is working her vintage #DollyParton tee. I can't even handle the cuteness!" Simpson, 33, wrote via Instagram. In a split screen, Simpson and fiance Eric Johnson's beautiful baby girl is seen posing for her famous mommy in both a black and white and color picture.

Maxwell's fashionable ensemble is detailed with a head shot of the 68-year-old country legend on the back and "Dolly Parton" written in hot pink. The little tot also had her blonde locks down with a simple braid on one side, and matched the chic look with black pants and leopard-print flats.
