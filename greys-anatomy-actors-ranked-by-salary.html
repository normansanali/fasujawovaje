We've Ranked The Most Popular Grey's Anatomy Actors, By Salary

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Grey's Anatomy is the longest-running medical drama on television, having debuted in 2005, and currently airing its 16th season. The show centers around protagonist, Meredith Grey, and her experiences as a surgeon at the Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital in Seattle.

The show has seen quite a turnaround of actors over the past fifteen years; the characters of Meredith Grey, Alex Karev, Miranda Bailey, and Richard Webber are the only cast members who have appeared in all sixteen seasons. Not to break any hearts, but Alex Karev is also leaving. Season sixteen will be his last on the Shonda Rhimes hit show.

Ellen Pompeo, who plays Meredith Grey, makes over $575,000 per episode. With a yearly income of close to $20 million, she is by far the top paid actress on the show.

15 Isaiah Washington: $500,000

Isaiah Washington had a rather dishonorable exit from Grey's Anatomy. At the end of season 3, he was dismissed for making homophobic comments to fellow cast member T.R. Knight. Creator Shonda Rhimes refused to accept him back, even after he publicly apologized. He played Dr. Preston Burke, and has a net worth of about $500,000.

14 Camilla Luddington: $1.3 Million

Camilla Luddington first appeared as a recurring cast member in season 9, and became a regular in season 10. She plays Jo Karev, wife of Alex Karev. In real life, British actress Camilla Luddington is married to actor Matthew Alan, and has a net worth of approximately $1.3 Million.

13 Kevin McKidd: $2 Million

Kevin McKidd plays Dr. Owen Hunt, and has been a regular cast member since the show's fifth season. He has touched millions of viewers with his portrayal of a soldier suffering from PTSD. Prior to landing a role on the show, he was best known for playing the character Tommy in Trainspotting. His net worth sits at about $2 Million.

12 Sarah Drew: $3 Million

Sarah Drew played the role of Dr. April Kepner, who was first introduced as a recurring cast member in season 6, and a regular cast member from season 7 until her departure in season 14. Before joining the cast, she played the role of Hannah in Everwood, from 2004-2006. Her net worth is $3 Million.

11 Martin Henderson: $6 Million

Martin Henderson has most recently appeared in the Netflix Original series Virgin River, but he's also a Grey's Anatomy alum! He played Dr. Nathan Riggs, fiance to Megan Hunt, Owen Hunt's sister. Though his character only appeared in seasons 12-14, he left a lasting impression on fans. He has a net worth of $6 Million.

10 Eric Dane: $7 Million

Eric Dane played the role of Dr. Mark Sloan, aka, 'McSteamy', from seasons two through nine on the show. He started his Grey's career as a guest star, and became a regular cast member in season 3. A reputed actor, he has a net worth of over $7 Million.

9 James Pickens Jr. $10 Million

James Pickens Jr. is one of the original cast members that has appeared in every season of the show. As Dr. Richard Webber, he was the long-running Chief of Surgery, until his successor, Dr. Derek Shepherd, takes over in the second season. The esteemed actor has a net worth of $10 Million.

8 Chandra Wilson: $10 Million

Chandra Wilson is one of the most renowned actresses on Grey's Anatomy, playing the role of Dr. Miranda Bailey. She has been a regular cast member for the entirety of the series, and there are no talks of her leaving any time soon! Her net worth is about $10 Million. 

7 Jesse Williams: $12 Million

Jesse Williams plays one of the most beloved doctors on set, Dr. Jackson Avery. He first arrived on set as a recurring cast member in season 6, and became a regular in season 7. He has signed on to appear in season 17, so we aren't losing another favorite! His net worth is $12 Million.

6 Justin Chambers: $18 Million

Justin Chambers plays the adored Dr. Alex Karev, a character that has evolved from childish attending, to interim chief of Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital. Though he is retiring after 15 years with the show, he leaves behind a legacy and adoring fans. He has said that he wants to spend more time with his wife and five children after a long run on the show.  His net worth is $18 Million. 

5 Kate Walsh: $20 Million

Kate Walsh played the role of Addison Montgomery, Derek Shepherd's ex-wife. She made several appearances in the first few seasons, and was the main protagonist on the spinoff show Private Practice. She hasn't made an appearance on the show since season 8, but who knows what the future might hold? Her net worth is $20 Million. 

4 Sandra Oh: $25 Million

Sandra Oh played the role of Dr. Cristina Yang, best friend and ally to Meredith Grey. Her departure from the show at the end of season 10 shocked and saddened fans. Despite having had a great run, she wanted to explore different acting roles and opportunities. Her net worth is $25 Million.

3 Katherine Heigl: $30 Million

There has been much speculation that the reason for Katherine Heigl's departure from the show came down to her bad attitude and inability to work well with her co-stars. Though not confirmed, Heigl, who played Dr. Izzie Stevens, has had a history of bed behaviour on set. Despite this, she is a very talented and successful actress, with a net worth of $30 Million.

2 Patrick Dempsey: $60 Million

Patrick Dempsey played the iconic role of Dr. Derek Shepherd until his tragic on-screen death in season 11. As Meredith's husband, partner, and biggest cheerleader, his departure made a big splash in the script and changed Meredith forever. He continues to act, and has been spending more time with his family and car collection. He has a net worth of $60 Million.

1 Ellen Pompeo: $70 Million

Ellen Pompeo is the highest paid actress on Grey's Anatomy, and rightfully so. She has been a constant for the entirety of the series, and her role is an integral part of the storyline. With a salary of approximately $575,000 per episode, she is making quite a good salary! Her net worth is $70 Million.
