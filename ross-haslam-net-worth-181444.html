About

English diver who is known for competing in the Commonwealth Games. He is known for his association with the City of Sheffield Diving Club and competes in the 3m category. He shares photos from his training as well as his competitive events with his over 30,000 Instagram followers. 

Before Fame

With a passion for the sport early on, he gained recognition for earning both bronze and silver medals at the Junior European Championships. He competed in his first professional event in 2015 at the European Games in Baku where he earned Silver in the man's synchronized 3m springboard event. 

Trivia

He won three different gold medals at the British Championships in 2017. 

Family Life

He was born and raised in Sheffield, England. He competed alongside his brother Jack at multiple events and competitions at the Optus Aquatic Centre.
