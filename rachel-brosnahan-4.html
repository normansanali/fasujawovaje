Career

Rachel Brosnahan started her career as an actress in the American film and television industry. In the year 2009, she made her acting debut in the movie titled The Unborn in which she played the role of Lisa and become popular. At that time she was studying in her High School.

Later in the same year, she also made her stage debut with her appearance in the popular play titled Up at Steppenwolf Theatre in Deerfield. Since then she has played an important role in many popular plays.

In the year 2011, she has worked in the movie Coming Up Roses and since then she has made her appearance in many popular movies like A New York Heartbeat, James White, The Finest Hours, I’m Your Woman and many other popular movies.

After that in the year 2013, she made her Broadway debut with her appearance in The Big Knife with Roundabout Theatre Company in Manhattan. She also worked in many short films like Adrift, Care, Basically, The Smut Locker, Fifteen Years Later, and many other short movies.

She also works as a producer and produced renowned feature film like I’m Your Woman and the television series Yearly Departed.

She is known for her role as Miriam “Midge” Maisel in the Amazon Prime Video series “The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel.”

In 2021, she reprised her role in the fourth season of “The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel” and also starred in the Netflix film “I’m Your Woman.”
