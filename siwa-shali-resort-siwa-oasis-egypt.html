Siwa Shali Resort, Siwa Oasis, Egypt

Publish date: 2024-08-28
Removed from everything and everyone, a holiday to discover another Egypt.
An Oasis where the local traditions, the ancient language and the crafts of the ancestors have endured to this day.
A desert where water flows out from an immense fossil basin in a thousand of pure streams.
Who arrives to Siwa cannot miss a visit to the majestic temple of Amon, to the ancient city of Shali, to the royal pool of Cleopatra’s Bath, to the sumptuously frescoed tombs and to the vast salt lakes that extend for miles into the desert and at least a day must be devoted to a safari in the desert to be overwhelmed by the magnitude of the Great Sea of Sands.

Address:

Siwa Shali Resort

Qesm Siwah, Matrouh Governorate, Egypt,
Siwa Oasis,
