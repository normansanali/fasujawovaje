However, the concept of “Benjamin Franklin Net Worth 2024” is an anachronism, as Franklin lived in the 18th century. Nonetheless, for the purpose of this hypothetical exercise, let’s explore what his net worth might look like if he were alive and thriving in the year 2024.

Franklin’s Inventions and Patents

Benjamin Franklin was a prolific inventor, creating devices such as the lightning rod, bifocal glasses, and the Franklin stove. In today’s market, patents for such inventions would be worth millions, if not billions, of dollars. Let’s explore how these inventions would contribute to his net worth.

  • Lightning Rod: A key invention that would have earned Franklin significant royalties.
  • Bifocal Glasses: With the aging global population, bifocals would be in high demand.
  • Franklin Stove: Energy efficiency is a major market, and this invention would be highly valued.

Franklin’s Writings and Royalties

As an author, Franklin’s works, including “Poor Richard’s Almanack” and his autobiography, have been read by millions. In the modern era, the royalties from these bestsellers would contribute substantially to his net worth.

  • Poor Richard’s Almanack: A yearly publication that would have generated continuous income.
  • Autobiography: A classic piece of literature that would earn significant royalties from book sales.

Real Estate and Investments
