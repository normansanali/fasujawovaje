TNT Title Bout Set For AEW Dynamite: St. Patricks Day Slam On 3/16

Publish date: 2024-08-01

All Elite Wrestling is bringing back the “St. Patrick’s Day Slam” edition of .

In a tweet on March 4, AEW President/CEO Tony Khan announced the news; the show will be held in the Freeman Coliseum in San Antonio, TX, on March 16.

This is a huge weekend for @AEW with #AEWRampage on @TNTdrama Tonight + REVOLUTION on Sunday! Tonight we’ll learn whom the 6th & final Face of the Revolution competitor will be! The ladder match winner will challenge the TNT Champ @ the St. Patrick’s Day Slam in San Antonio 3/16! pic.twitter.com/7xn5HU5GfT

— Tony Khan (@TonyKhan) March 5, 2022

In the same tweet, Khan stated that the winner of the Face of the Revolution Ladder Match will challenge for the TNT Championship on this special episode of Dynamite. Sammy Guevara currently holds the title, and he successfully defended it on the March 4 episode of AEW Dynamite. Guevara will put the championship on the line again on the March 9 episode of AEW Dynamite; there, Scorpio Sky will challenge for the title.

The 2021 edition of “St. Patrick’s Day Slam” featured the classic Unsanctioned Lights Out Match between Thunder Rosa and Dr. Britt Baker, D.M.D.
