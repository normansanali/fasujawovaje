Jimmy Fallon And Taylor Swift Played Name That Song

Host Jimmy Fallon of The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon! has had several hilarious, emotional, awkward and memorable moments on his show. Not every guest seems to like the host when on the show, like when Martin Short threw shade at Fallon during their interview.

The host does have several celebrity friends, including star Taylor Swift. The star once surprised Fallon with an incredibly emotional moment on The Tonight Show, nearly bringing him to tears.

When Taylor Swift made another visit to The Tonight Show to have a chat with Jimmy Fallon, the two played a game called "name that song."

Before the host and Swift started playing, Fallon explained the rules: "The Roots are gonna start playing a song, one instrument at a time. Taylor and I can buzz in and guess as soon as we know the song. But if you guess wrong, the other person gets a chance to steal."

"Treacherous, crazy." the singer joked. Fallon, chuckling added, "You can play along at home," before they got started.

Once the game started, The Roots (The Tonight Show's house band and Grammy-winning legends in hip-hop), began playing the first song.

The first song, Fallon guessed wrong, with "Oops... I Did it Again by Britney Spears". However, he got the singer right, and Swift quickly figured out the song, Spears' hit, Baby One More Time. "It's them Max Martin chords!" Swift said, laughing with Fallon.

After Swift won the first round, Fallon felt Swift would definitely beat him at the game, commenting, "I'm so mad! Alright. Ugh. Of course Taylor's gonna kill me at this." The Shake It Off singer smiled and agreed.

As the two battled back and forth, Swift got several songs right some almost immediately, telling Fallon "It's the chords," which helped her recognize certain songs. Fallon also got some right, including one of Swift's own songs!

Taylor Swift Failed To Recognize Her Own Song, Shake It Off

While Taylor Swift and Jimmy Fallon were playing "name that song" on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon!, Swift surprised Fallon by recognizing certain songs quickly.

When they got to the final song however, it was pretty surprising that Swift didn't recognize it, especially considering it was one of her own hits!

The host announced, "All right, it's time for the final song," joking that "this is worth 10,000 points." When Swift asked, "Towards what?" Fallon said, "It's anyone's game. Whoever wins this, wins the whole game," which brought laughter from the audience.

As The Roots began playing, Swift listened, but didn't seem to recognize the song they were playing. As Fallon listened, he was leaned down over the buzzer, his hand above it.

As more instruments joined in, he glanced up, seeming to know the song, and buzzed in. He guessed, "Shake It Off." Swift's face remained completely unchanged until it was revealed the host was correct.

At first the star seemed almost shocked, breaking out into a smile, starting to laugh, but she also seemed a little embarrassed too, as Fallon danced around the stage, grinning and yelling, "Yeah! Haha! Yeah!"

Glancing down, she turned to walk off the stage.

Did Jimmy Fallon Cause Taylor Swift To Storm Off The Stage?

After Swift was able to recognize multiple songs within moments, she seemed stunned that she hadn't recognized her own work. As the host danced around his guest in victory, Swift looked down, turned around and began walking off the stage.

It seemed as though Swift was a little upset she hadn't recognized her song. As Fallon continued celebrating that he'd beaten her, and she walked away, Fallon pointed at her walking and laughed, then quickly ran over to her to stop her.

Before she could reach the curtain, Fallon reached her. Still laughing, he said, "I'm sorry. No, no, no. Come on." As he put his arm around Swift and brought her back on the stage, he added, "That's my favorite thing that ever happened on the whole show ever!"
