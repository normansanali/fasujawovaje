Eric Benet, Wife Manuela Testolini Debut Baby Daughter Amoura: Photo

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Too cute! Eric Benet spent his Valentine’s Day posing for a series of sweet family portraits and Us Weekly has an exclusive look at his baby daughter Amoura Luna. The R&B singer and his wife Manuela Testolini welcomed the new addition to their family this past July.

The family shoot, which Benet set up as a gift to his wife Testolini for the romantic holiday, features all his ladies, including daughter Lucia and 23-year-old daughter India, from a previous relationship.

As Us exclusively reported over the summer, their little girl received the middle name “Luna" because she was born on the night of a super moon.
