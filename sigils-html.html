Sigils - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-17

Online gaming personality who has gained fame for his Sigils YouTube channel. He has earned massive popularity for his Fortnite gaming playthroughs, streaming highlights, Let's Plays, and Minecraft content. He has also posted a number of videos about the popular Among Us game. 

Before Fame

He launched his YouTube channel in April 2012. 

Trivia

He has seen his YouTube audience grow to more than 1.2 million subscribers. He has had a secondary YouTube channel at SigilsTV. He has also streamed Fortnite gaming sessions to his Siglis Twitch channel. In October of 2020, he posted a picture of his new Xbox Series X console that was sent to him for free by Xbox. 

Family Life

His real name is Andrew.

Associated With

He and Ian Stapleton have gamed playing Fortnite together on his YouTube channel.
