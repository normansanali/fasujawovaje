Bayleys First Remarks After Kairi Sanes WWE Crown Jewel Return

Publish date: 2024-09-19

Bayley has a great relationship with a lot of people in WWE and she continues to be a leader for Damage CTRL. Kairi Sane made her shocking return and aided Iyo Sky during Crown Jewel yesterday and now Bayley made her first comments after Sane’s return.

Ringside News provided full coverage of WWE Crown Jewel in Saudi Arabia, which comprised solid matches and shocking moments throughout the night. This included Kairi Sane making her shocking return to WWE television after three years and assaulting Bianca Belair – costing her the match against Iyo Sky for the Women’s Championship.

Bayley took to Twitter and made her first comments after Kairi Sane’s return, as she tagged Dakota Kai and told her Damage CTRL member to call her back to discuss matters. In case you didn’t know, Bayley was the one who sent Kairi Sane away from the company for 3 years back in 2020 in their storyline.

”@ImKingKota I’m back in the states from Saudi. Call me back”

Kairi Sane has been added to the Smackdown roster following her return. WWE is also bringing back old history with Kairi Sane’s Crown Jewel return. It remains to be seen how WWE will book Kairi Sane now that she has returned to WWE television.
