Raquel Pennington Defended Amber Heard During Her Court Case With Johnny Depp

Pennington would take part in the court case via video deposition. Pennington spoke about the night in which things got physical between Depp and Heard.

“I thought it was a really bad idea, but you can’t stop somebody from coming over to their own home,” she said in her deposition.

According to Pennington's statement, Depp was yelling, while Heard was asking for help, something she never did.

“She was calling for help, and that had never happened before,” Pennington said about Heard. “Saying, ‘Help me, help me.’ I went and I put my hands up on his chest and I was like, ‘Stop, just stop."

Pennington would make another interesting reveal, stating that the two were no longer close friends and had drifted apart. So what happened between the two? In short, an argument over Thanksgiving dishes...

Pennington Revealed A Fall Out Took Place Over Thanksgiving Dishes

Surely, there has to have been a build up of some sort prior... and the two didn't just end their relationship over dish options. However, Pennington pointed at the moment as a big reason for their friendship ending. According to the Raquel, things got physical between the two because of a dish dispute.

"We were getting ready for Thanksgiving and we were looking for maybe some glasses or china and couldn't find them anywhere," Pennington said.

"She finally found them in a place that I thought she had been looking for and we started arguing about it. She thought I wasn't looking hard enough and I told her I thought I had looked there," the woman added.

Raquel further stated that it was the only time things got physically heated between the two. It would cause the end of their friendship and there is no word on if the two were able to repair things in the recent years.
