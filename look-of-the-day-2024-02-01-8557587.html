Kim Kardashian Wore the Indisputable Wardrobe Staple I Swear By

Publish date: 2024-08-24

In my opinion, Kim Kardashian has a chokehold on pop culture. Whether she’s debuting kitschy lingerie (anyone up for trying that candy bra?) or showing off her underwear, she’s usually the talk of the town. So if you thought I’d pass up my chance to wax poetic about her sexy cowgirl-inspired look, you’re wrong. 

Kardashian took to Instagram and posted a carousel of casual photos captioned, “Not my first rodeo.” She wore a tiny ribbed tank top with a raw hemline, black sweatpants, and a cowboy hat. You probably have some iteration of this outfit in your wardrobe; it’s a classic lounge ‘fit that’s equal parts fashionable and cute. But have you considered using the basic white tank as a layering staple during the cold-weather months?

Ugg Adrianne Crop Tank Top

Hanes Cotton Jersey Tank Top

This Ugg Adrianne crop tank serves as the perfect example. It has a scoop neck, supportive bust, and comfortable, form-fitting fit. Pop it under an oversized knit sweater to stop wind flow and keep you warm, or layer a thick cardigan over the piece for a style alternative. This $10 Hanes find is great under form-fitting long-sleeve shirts. The base creates a smooth, flattering look without being overly bulky. And if it gets too hot, simply remove one of the tops; that’s the beauty of layering. 

Kardashian-inspired tank tops are great to pair with see-through tops, too. I love my Intimissimi cashmere shirt that Kendall Jenner has worn, but it’s slightly sheer. If I’m looking for more coverage, a white cami fixes the problem.

I don’t have to tell you this, but uncomplicated white tops aren’t just for layering. It’s a multi-season staple you can lounge in all year long. I own my fair share, and I’ve worn them for everything from exercising and sleeping to cooking and lounging; they’re my fashion ride or die. Not to mention, they’re timeless and ageless; just look at Jennifer Lopez, Hailey Bieber, and now Kim K, all of whom have worn the trusty staple on repeat, as proof.   

Snag your own white tank for winter layering and beyond from Amazon, J.Crew, and Nordstrom.
