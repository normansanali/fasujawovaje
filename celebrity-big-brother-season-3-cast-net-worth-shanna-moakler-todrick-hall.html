'Celebrity Big Brother' Season 3's Official Cast, Ranked By Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-09-25

Big Brother is back! After waiting for what seems like forever, we finally have the cast for Celebrity Big Brother season 3. Julie Chen Moonves gave fans hints on January 26 on Twitter, and the cast was finally revealed that night during The Amazing Race.

Celebrity Big Brother will premiere on February 2 at 8/7c and air on every night, except Tuesdays, until the finale on February 23. The ten house guests this year include a former basketball player, an Olympic figure skater, a Saturday Night Live star and a rockstar's daughter.

In the past, the winner, runner-up and Favorite Houseguest have all walked away with the same amount of money as the regular seasons, all for a charity of their choosing, of course. The celebrity season is still hosted by Chen-Moonves and will run alongside the Winter Olympics.

Here's the cast of the third season of Celebrity Big Brother and how much they're worth already.

11 Todd Bridges' Net Worth Is $250,000

Todd Bridges is an American actor, best known for his role of Willis Jackson on Diff'rent Strokes. He also had recurring roles on the shows Monk and Everybody Hates Chris. From 2008 to 2013, Bridges was the commentator on TruTv Presents: World's Dumbest. The 56-year-old appeared in other TV shows, movies and music video and is the last surviving original actor of Diff'rent Strokes. According to Celebrity Net Worth,he is estimated to be worth $250,000.

10 Mirai Nagasu's Net Worth Is About $500,000

Mirai Nagasu is a Japanese- American figure skater, who competed in the 2018 Winter Olympic Games. She is an Olympic Bronze Medalist and the first woman to land the triple axel in an Olympic Game. The 28-year-old is a three-time Four Continents medalist and two-time World Junior medalist. She is the youngest on the cast and may have an advantage, due to her age and athletic ability. Due to her time in the Olympics, ads and sponsorships, her net worth is estimated to be $500,000, according to many websites, but there is no definitive answer.

9 Cynthia Bailey-Hill's Net Worth Is $2.5 Million

Cynthia Bailey-Hill is a model, reality TV personality and actress. Her net worth is $2.5 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Reportedly, she makes $300,000 per season on The Real Housewives of Atlanta. She has been married to sportscaster Mike Hill since 2020. Bailey-Hill, 54, has appeared on various TV shows and music videos.

8 Todrick Hall's Net Worth Is $4 Million

Todrick Hall was one of those rumored house guests, and now he is joining the season. Hall is a self-proclaimed super fan of the show, so it will be interesting to see how he plays the game. He is a dancer, singer, actor, director and choreographer who is best known for his time on American Idol and RuPaul's Drag Race. The 36-year-old is also a content creator on YouTube and a Broadway star. His net worth is estimated to be $4 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

7 Chris Kattan's Net Worth Is $6 Million

Chris Kattan is best known for his time as a cast member on Saturday Night Live. He is also a comedian and author and actor. Some of his roles include Night at the Roxbury, Christmas in Wonderland, The Middle and more. The 51-year-old competed on Dancing with theStars and revealed he suffered a neck injury years prior, which limited his mobility. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Kattan's net worth is estimated to be $6 million.

6 Miesha 'Cupcake' Tate's Net Worth Is $6 Million

Miesha "Cupcake" Tate is estimated to be worth $6 million. She earned that money from doing professional mixed martial arts. Tate competes in the UFC and goes by the name "Cupcake." The 45-year-old is a former UFC Women's Bantamweight Champion. As of 2021, she is number eight in the UFC Women's Bantam rankings. Her strength, passion and toughness is what may take her far in the game.

5 Carson Kressley's Net Worth Is $8 Million

With a net worth of $8 million, Carson Kressley is best known for his time on Queer Eye For The Straight Guy, starting in 2003. The 52-year-old has also starred in numerous movies and TV shows including Dancing With The Stars, The Perfect Man, 16 to Life and more. He is an Emmy Award winner, author, fashion designer and icon for the LGBTQ+ community.

4 Chris Kirkpatrick's Net Worth Is $10 Million

Chris Kirkpatrick is saying "Bye, Bye, Bye" to his wife and son and saying hello to the Big Brother house for a month. The *NSNYC founder and member is joining the cast. *NSYNC sold over 50 million records worldwide. Sadly, the band broke up in 2002, but they all remain close. Kirkpatrick is also known for voicing Chip Skylark in the Nickelodeon show, The Fairly Odd Parents. He has also starred in music videos and minor acting roles. All of this has made him a net worth of $10 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

3 Teddi Mellencamp's Net Worth Is $12 Million

Teddi Mellencamp, also known as Teddi Jo, is the daughter of John "Cougar" Mellencamp. She joined the cast of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills in season 8. The 40-year-old is married to Edwin Arroyave, CEO of Skyline Security. She earns most of her money from reality TV and is estimated to be worth $12 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Teddi and her husband bought a house that cost $4.07 Million in 2018. Their previous home cost $1.8 million. Their real estate factored into their net worth, as well.

2 Shanna Moakler's Net Worth Is $15 Million

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Shanna Moakler's net worth is $15 million. She earned that money from being a model, actress, reality TV star and former Miss USA. Moakler was the Playmate of the Month for Playboy in 2001 and appeared on the series Pacific Blue and Meet The Barkers, which she appeared on with her then-husband, Travis Barker, drummer in Blink-182. The 46-year-old started competing in pageants at 17, where she won the Miss Teen All American Title, Miss New York USA and was the first runner-up in the Miss USA pageant.

1 Lamar Odom's Net Worth Is $30 Million

Lamar Odom was also one of the rumored house guests for weeks, and now it's finally true! He is a former American basketball player and the ex-husband of Khloe Kardashian. As a former athlete, Odom may be good at a lot of the physical challenges on this season. He was married to Kardashian from 2009 to 2016. He has three children from his former girlfriend, Liza Morales. He may be one of the richest cast members with a net worth of $30 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth, with an annual salary of about $8.2 million.
